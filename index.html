Questions from PPC Teams

We built Ada to work alongside PPC professionals, not replace them. Here are the questions we hear most.

Will Ada cannibalize my existing campaigns?

No. Ada runs from a completely separate Google Ads account, targeting queries and placements where your ads are not showing. She uses last-click attribution, so if your ad gets the click, you owe Ada nothing. Her entire purpose is incremental revenue.

Will Ada compete with my bids and raise my CPCs?

No. Ada specifically avoids auctions where your ads are already showing. She targets the gaps in your coverage. Your CPCs stay exactly where they are.

I want full control over my campaigns.

You keep it. Ada runs her own layer. You can give her your entire catalog or limit her to specific products. Add negative keywords and exclusions any time. Your existing campaigns remain 100% under your control.

How do I verify the attribution is accurate?

Ada uses last-click paid attribution via her conversion pixel. You see every order attributed to Ada in your dashboard, complete with order IDs you can cross-reference with your own data. Full transparency.

What access does Ada need? Is it safe?

Ada needs read access to your Merchant Center feed. She runs campaigns from her own Google Ads account, not yours. She never touches your existing campaigns or ad account.

What types of campaigns does Ada run?

Google Shopping only. Ada does not write ad copy or create display ads. Shopping campaigns are data-driven by design: your product feed determines what shows. Ada optimizes bids, targeting, and product selection based on performance data.

Is this really cancel anytime or am I locked in?

No contracts. No minimums. No lock-in. You can pause or cancel Ada at any time. If you stop, Ada stops. You only ever pay commission on orders she has already generated.

How much will Ada spend on ads?

Ada invests based on performance. She scales back when campaigns are not working and doubles down when they are. There is no fixed budget; she optimizes for profitable incremental sales. You never see the ad spend bill, only the commission on orders she generates.
